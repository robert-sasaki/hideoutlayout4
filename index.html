The Hideout is downtown Austin's oldest independent coffee house. We are open morning to night, weekdays and weekends and serve beer, wine, fresh sandwiches, pastries, ice cream, as well as an assortment of beverages and coffee drinks. We also proudly feature baked goods from Easy Tiger! You can bring food and drink from the coffee house up into the theatre to enjoy during a performance!
